Milton Featherson

Founder and CEO of BrownBeat Entertainment.

From the streets of Eldorado Park South, of Johannesburg South Africa, a previously disadvantaged background could not stop him from catapulting into the industry of music and media. From lead vocalist of Top South African dance band, Dr. Victor and the Rasta Rebels, his musical career made it possible to travel nationally and internationally where he performed alongside international as well as local artists. To his credit as an accredited broadcast sound engineer, he has mixed a number of intense live transmissions of World Events such Football World Cups, Olympics, with SABC (South African Broadcast Corporation) and MNET SUPERSPORT. His critical knowledge and technical skills in Audio Visual Systems allowed him to contribute in the Consultation, Design, Procurement and Installation of a Broadcast standard Radio Studio (Kofifi FM 97.2) Johannesburg South Africa.
